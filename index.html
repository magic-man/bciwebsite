Bitcoin Interest?

Bitcoin Interest ("BCI") is a competitive staking cryptocurrency focusing on three key areas Technology, Community, and Savings. Our blockchain has new integrated savings technology that allows our users to safely park their coins and earn interest payments without ever moving coins from their wallets..

Simply register your Wallet Address and the amount of Bitcoin Interest coins it contains to begin receiving weekly and monthly interest payments.

Interest Payments

*Graphic just for demonstration purposes.

We figured out a new way to generate interest payments to reward our users through what we call "Enhanced Proof of Work" using the Equihash algorithm.

Every time a miner solves a block in the Bitcoin Interest network, they are rewarded 10.26 BCI coins while an additional 3.24 BCI is generated from the block and sent to the "Interest Pool." Once the active Weekly or Monthly interest round has finished, the Interest Pool is distributed evenly to all of the users participating in the current interest round.

If 5,000 blocks were solved last month, then the Interest Pool would contain 5,000 BCI coins that would be distributed to the participating users at the end of the Monthly interest cycle.

Miners are rewarded for stabilizing the network while users are rewarded for creating a less volatile market by saving their coins. It's a win, win situation for everyone.

Unlike other interest payment platforms that require you to send your coins to them, Bitcoin Interest is the first fully decentralized interest payments platform that will never ask you to send your coins anywhere!

With our advanced interest technology you can hold your coins in your own wallet and receive interest payments directly into your wallet with absolutely no risk to you. You'll never be required to trust your coin holdings with anyone again!

What is Equinox?

Equinox is our version 2 of Bitcoin Interest where we have made a massive overhaul of the coin and everything that goes with it. The biggest changes being the move to ProgPoW and Segregated pools.

Equinox features will begin to roll out at the end of September. We are going to spend the first couple of weeks ensuring our new wallet and interest is functioning as intended. Once that is insured new features will roll out like new pools, insurance, and address checking.

What did you change?

  • Changing from Equihash to ProgPoW
  • Interest is getting better with Segregated Pools
  • Mining reward for solving a block will no longer be halved
  • Proof of Ownership
  • Smaller Blockchain
